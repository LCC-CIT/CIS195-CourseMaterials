Our dog, Sugar, likes to eat whatever the kids are snacking on. Is it OK for the dog to eat chocoloate?
Chocolate is toxic to doges. Please do not feed your dog chocolate. Try planning a game with your children - when you feed them people treats, they can feed Sugar dog treats.
